GW CS Senior Design 2016

Abed Kassem: MyPerfectMeal

MyPerfectMeal (MPM) is an iOS based mobile application designed and developed to solve common problems many face while dining out. MPM provides its users with personalized meal suggestions from local restaurants guaranteed to be delicious and memorable! The application is able to find personal meal recommendations based on the user's personal profile.

Bio: Originally from Beirut, Lebanon I moved to the US when I was in my early teens. I was introduced to programming late in high school and fell in love with it ever since. I plan on working around the DMV area after graduation.
